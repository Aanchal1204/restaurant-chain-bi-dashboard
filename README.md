🍽️ Affordable Business Intelligence for Small & Mid-Sized Restaurant Chains

🎯 Business Problem

While large restaurant chains often have access to dedicated analytics teams and enterprise Business Intelligence platforms, small and medium-sized restaurant businesses typically operate with limited visibility into their data.

Many growing restaurant brands rely on spreadsheets and manual reporting, making it difficult to identify:

* Which outlets are truly profitable
* Which menu items drive revenue
* How much inventory wastage occurs daily
* Whether margins are improving over time
* Which operational issues require immediate attention

This project was designed as an affordable and scalable Business Intelligence solution for small and mid-sized restaurant chains, enabling owners and managers to make data-driven decisions without requiring a dedicated analytics team.
